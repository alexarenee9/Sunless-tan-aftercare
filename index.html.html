The First 24 Hours
🚿
Hours 1–12, stay dry
No water, no sweat, no gym. The longer you wait, the deeper the color. Stay in loose, dark clothing until you rinse. Rinse when Alexa told you to! 😄 Not sure? Text me: 317-416-3654
💧
First rinse, be gentle
Lukewarm water only, no soap, no washcloth, no scrubbing. Rinse until no more bronze water comes off. Looks lighter after? Don't panic! Color is still developing and will deepen over the next few hours.
🧴
Right after, moisturize
Apply moisturizer while skin is still slightly damp. Regular lotion in the AM, gradual extender at night for a longer, better-fading glow. Do not skip this.
2
Do's & Hard No's

✓ yes, do these things

  • ✓Moisturize morning and night, every single day. Twice a day if you want extra credit! 🙌
  • ✓Use a sulfate-free, tan-safe body wash. We carry BeetBod in studio or check our Amazon picks below!
  • ✓Pat dry after every shower, no rubbing, ever
  • ✓Dust baby powder or sweat powder in sweaty areas and where straps or clothing rub. Helps everything fade evenly!
  • ✓Apply SPF when you're in the sun, your tan is not sun protection!
  • ✓Shave the day before your appointment, then wait 24 hours after your first rinse before shaving again

✕ hard no's, we're serious

  • ✕Exfoliating, scrubbing, or loofahs of any kind, your tan will peace out
  • ✕Long hot baths or hot tubs, a hard no every time
  • ✕Products with alcohol, AHAs, BHAs, or retinol
  • ✕Tight or synthetic fabrics right after, friction = patchy. Protect your investment!
  • ✕Swimming , chlorine and salt water will wreck it fast. SweatGuard helps, but still pat dry and moisturize right after!
✨ things your tan wishes you knew ✨

Give extra love when moisturizing your elbows and knees (front AND back!), inner arm bends, under your chest, and ankles. These spots fold, rub, and dry out the fastest. A little extra product there keeps your fade looking even and gorgeous.
