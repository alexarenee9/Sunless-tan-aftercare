<div class="section-head">⏰ 12 to 24 Hours Before</div>
<div class="card-area">

  <div class="info-card">
    <div class="info-card-head">
      <div class="time-badge badge-pink">STEP 1</div>
      <div class="card-title">Wash Hair First</div>
    </div>
    <div class="card-body">
      <ul>
        <li>Wash and condition hair before anything else
          <span class="cheeky">Conditioner on your skin is basically a tan repellent. Get it all out first.</span>
        </li>
        <li>Hair needs to be fully dry before your appointment
          <span class="cheeky">Wet drips on fresh tan = streaks. We're not doing that.</span>
        </li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="info-card">
    <div class="info-card-head">
      <div class="time-badge badge-orange">STEP 2</div>
      <div class="card-title">Shave and Wax</div>
    </div>
    <div class="card-body">
      <ul>
        <li>Shave before you exfoliate, not after
          <span class="cheeky">Order matters here. Don't freestyle this one.</span>
        </li>
        <li>Waxing must be done at least 24 hours before
          <span class="cheeky">Fresh wax on fresh tan is a recipe for patchy chaos.</span>
        </li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="info-card">
    <div class="info-card-head">
      <div class="time-badge badge-yellow">STEP 3</div>
      <div class="card-title">Exfoliate Like You Mean It</div>
    </div>
    <div class="card-body">
      <ul>
        <li>Use a pH-balanced, oil-free scrub or dry exfoliation gloves</li>
        <li>Really get into knees, elbows, ankles, feet and armpits
          <span class="cheeky">These spots hoard dead skin like it's their job.</span>
        </li>
        <li>Beet-It Scrub available at the studio!
          <span class="cheeky">Hint: cheaper in studio. Just saying.</span>
        </li>
      </ul>
      <div class="tip"><strong>Think of it like sanding wood before you paint.</strong> You wouldn't skip the sanding. Don't skip this.</div>
      <div class="tip" style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>No scrub at home?</strong> Mix sugar and water into a paste and scrub in circles. DO NOT grab whatever soap is sitting in your shower. Most leave oils that mess with your tan. Seriously, please don't.</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="info-card">
    <div class="info-card-head">
      <div class="time-badge badge-pink">STEP 4</div>
      <div class="card-title">Rinse and Stay Clean</div>
    </div>
    <div class="card-body">
      <ul>
        <li>No lotion, perfume or scented products after your shower
          <span class="cheeky">Your skin needs to be a blank canvas. Clean and ready.</span>
        </li>
        <li>Need to rinse again? Do it 4+ hours before your appointment</li>
        <li>Light rinse only, no soap except where needed
          <span class="cheeky">You know where. Armpits. We all know.</span>
        </li>
      </ul>
      <div class="tip"><strong>Heads up:</strong> Hormones and medications can affect how much color your skin absorbs. Not your fault, just something to know!</div>
    </div>
  </div>

</div>

<div class="card-area" style="padding-top:4px;">
  <div class="pink-callout">
    <div class="pink-callout-label">✨ Things your skin wishes you knew ✨</div>
    <p>Give extra attention to <span>knees, elbows, ankles and armpits</span> when you exfoliate. These spots fold, dry out and cling to color the most. A little extra scrubbing there means a way more even tan. Worth the 30 extra seconds, we promise.</p>
  </div>
